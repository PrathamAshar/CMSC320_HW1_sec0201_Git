Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git allows users to track changes, collaborate with others, and revert to previous versions of a project if needed.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to healthcare. For example, hospitals use data science to analyze patient data and predict disease risk.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines two branches together and keeps both histories. Rebasing moves one branch on top of another to make the commit history appear linear.
